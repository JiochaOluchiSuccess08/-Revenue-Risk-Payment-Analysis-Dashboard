# -Revenue-Risk-Payment-Analysis-Dashboard
Revenue is only valuable when it is received — this project explores the gap between sales and cash flow.

📊 Revenue Risk & Payment Analysis Dashboard 

🔍 Overview

This project analyzes sales data with a focus on payment performance and revenue risk.
It goes beyond traditional sales reporting by incorporating payment delays and customer behavior, providing a more complete view of how revenue is realized.

🎯 Objectives

Evaluate revenue based on payment timing (On-time vs Delayed)
Identify customers contributing to payment delays
Analyze the impact of delayed payments on cash flow
Highlight high-risk regions and customer segments
Support better credit and collection decisions

📈 Key Metrics

Total Revenue: $8.3M
Delayed Revenue: $2.43M
On-Time Revenue: $5.87M
Average Payment Delay: 31 days

💡 Key Insights

Approximately 29% of total revenue is delayed, posing potential cash flow challenges
A small number of customers account for a large share of delayed payments
High-value customers contribute significantly to both revenue and payment delays
The United Kingdom represents the highest concentration of revenue at risk

📊 Dashboard Features

Revenue and payment KPIs
Payment status analysis (On-time vs Delayed)
Customer risk identification
Revenue at risk by country
Payment delay distribution

🛠 Tools & Technologies

Power BI (Data Visualization)
SQL (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
DAX (Measures & Calculations)

🚀 Business Value

This dashboard helps businesses:
Monitor cash flow risks linked to delayed payments
Identify high-risk customers early
Improve credit control and collection strategies
Make more informed financial and sales decisions

💼 Business Context

As a sales professional, I have experienced how meeting sales targets does not always translate to immediate cash flow.
This project reflects a real-world challenge — understanding not just how revenue is generated, but how and when it is actually received.

📁 Dataset

Retail sales dataset enhanced with:
Payment Date
Payment Status
Payment Delay
Customer Segmentation
